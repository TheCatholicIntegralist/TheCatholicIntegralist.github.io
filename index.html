Mission

Ad Triunum is a Catholic think tank dedicated to studying and promoting the social, political, philosophical, and theological traditions of historic Christianity.

Our work focuses on post-liberal political thought, Catholic integralism as a subject of intellectual inquiry, and traditional Catholic theology lived in fidelity to the Triune God and His Holy Church.

We seek to encourage thoughtful discussion, serious scholarship, and practical engagement rooted in the Catholic intellectual tradition. We believe ideas matter and that Christians are called to contribute to the common good through charity, truth, and faithful witness.

Principles

  • Fidelity to Sacred Scripture and Sacred Tradition.
  • Respect for the teaching authority of the Catholic Church.
  • Promotion of the dignity of the human person.
  • Study of Catholic social teaching and post-liberal political thought.
  • Defense of the family and the common good.
  • Serious intellectual formation grounded in reason and faith.

Our Work

We publish essays, educational resources, reading recommendations, historical studies, and commentary on theology, philosophy, history, and Catholic social thought.

Our long-term vision is to cultivate informed discussion and foster communities committed to living out Christian principles in public and private life.
